About

Hireability: The foundation of this project is a resume automation system.In the system NER, Regex, NLTK, and Spacy's parse matcher will extract necessary information such as Name, Address, Email, phone number, Nationality, Skills [Hard Skills, Soft Skills], Education, Experience, Experience Year, Languages etc from the CV in .json format.
